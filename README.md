Delivery Operations Analysis & Customer Clustering
(Real Company Case Study – Italy)

This project analyzes delivery operations of Foorban, a real Italian company providing smart fridges, coffee machines, and water dispensers to businesses.

The analysis focuses on delivery missions to identify operational inefficiencies, unusual courier behaviors, and customer segments with different service characteristics. By combining mission timelines, service durations, idle times, and geographic information, the project delivers actionable insights to support operational optimization and potential business expansion.


Key Topics

Courier performance and operational efficiency analysis
Customer clustering using KMeans
Idle time, cycle time, and mission timeline analysis
Geographic and hub-level insights
Interactive visualizations using Plotly
